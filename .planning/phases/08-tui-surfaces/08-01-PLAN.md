<objective>
Ship the first real Phase 8 operator surface: a minimal inbox overlay that makes the
already-shipped Phase 7 inbox model visible and actionable from the TUI.

Before this slice, unresolved inbox rows existed durably in store/compose/runtime layers,
but the TUI exposed them only indirectly through blocked DAG nodes and composer hints.
This plan adds a direct inbox surface without widening scope into merge-train UI,
transcript UI, or richer selection mechanics.
</objective>
